Execute este comando no Render Shell (terminal do backend):

psql $DATABASE_URL -c "
UPDATE products 
SET is_active = true, updated_at = NOW()
WHERE is_active = false
  AND (
    DATE(updated_at AT TIME ZONE 'UTC' AT TIME ZONE 'America/Sao_Paulo') = CURRENT_DATE
    OR DATE(created_at AT TIME ZONE 'UTC' AT TIME ZONE 'America/Sao_Paulo') = CURRENT_DATE
  );
"

OU execute o script Node.js:

cd backend && node --loader ./node_modules/.bin/esbuild-register src/scripts/restore-today-products.js
